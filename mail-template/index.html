Thank you again for choosing LoremApp. We're excited to help you get started. To begin, please activate your wallet:

  • Login to your LoremApp account.
  • Go to the “Account” section and select “KYC and Verification”.
  • Click on the “Tier 1 Verification” tab.
  • Paste your ID number in the text box and tap “Verify”.

Once verified, your unique wallet account number will be generated. You can then fund your wallet and access features like currency exchange or delivery bookings.

Also, don’t forget to join our online community for support and updates.
